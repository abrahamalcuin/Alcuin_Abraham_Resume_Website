Our Story

We met quietly, grew steadily, and learned to listen to the gentle promptings that led us toward each other. Here is a glimpse into the moments that shaped our relationship and prepared us for the covenants we will make on our wedding day.

From Quiet Hellos to Unmistakable Prompts

Our paths crossed at the Manila Institute of Religion where we both volunteered for a service project. A quick hello became a weekly conversation about classes, callings, and our favorite scriptures. There was no dramatic spark—just a growing sense of peace every time we spent time together.

Over shared jeepney rides and long walks after institute, we found matching priorities: faith before fear, family above busyness, and a deep desire to serve. Those little, ordinary choices built a friendship with extraordinary trust.

“When I am with you, I feel the same calm assurance I get inside the temple.” — Patricia

Milestones We Treasure

  1. March 2022 Manila Institute

    First Conversation

    We stayed late to stack chairs, and the tiny act of service turned into a two-hour conversation.

  2. July 2022 Quezon City

    Our Friendship Deepened

    We swapped devotionals, visited families in need, and realized our goals pointed in the same direction.

  3. May 2024 Baguio Retreat

    The Question

    During a rainy hike we finally voiced what we both felt: the Lord was asking us to build a life together.

  4. December 2024 Manila Temple

    Engaged

    Surrounded by Christmas lights and close family, Alcuin proposed after a temple session. Patricia said yes before he even finished the question.

Every mile in between taught us patience, covenant love, and a lot of joyful laughter.

Looking Ahead

We are excited for the quiet routines and big adventures ahead. We want a home where testimonies are shared over breakfast, music fills the kitchen, and friends always feel welcome. Most of all, we look forward to inviting the Savior into every plan we make.
